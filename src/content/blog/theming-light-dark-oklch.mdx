Modern CSS gives us a shockingly small surface area for full theming. This site
runs two completely different moods — sunset and midnight — from a handful of
custom properties.

## One source, sixteen colors

Open Props UI generates a full 16-step palette from a single
`--palette-source`. Swap the source with `light-dark()` and the whole ramp
re-tints:

```css
--palette-source: light-dark(
  oklch(0.62 0.13 55),
  /* burnt orange → cream tints */ oklch(0.52 0.06 62)
    /* warm brown → tan tints */
);
```

## Why OKLCH

Perceptual uniformity means the cream in light mode and the tan in dark mode
sit at predictable lightness steps. No more hand-tuning every shade.

That's the whole trick. The rest is just taste.
