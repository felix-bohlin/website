Welcome! This is the first post on my new site. Think of this one as the liner
notes: what it's built with and why.

## The stack

- **Astro** — zero JS by default, content collections for this very blog
- **Open Props UI** — my own component library, eating my own dog food
- **Fraunces** — a variable font with `SOFT` and `WONK` axes doing the heavy
  70s lifting
- **tiks** — procedural UI sounds, pure Web Audio synthesis

## The look

The palette is straight off a worn record sleeve: burnt orange, tomato red and
cream in light mode; denim blue, chocolate brown and tan after dark. All of it
flows through Open Props UI's theme tokens, so every component adapts without
extra CSS.
